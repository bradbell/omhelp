Latex Normal Characters

Syntax
$latex Character $$

Description
Display in Latex mode the character specified by Character where Character is any of the ascii characters included below:

Ascii Letters
The ascii characters from A through Z and from a through z. For example 
 
     abcdefghijklmnopqrstuvwxyz
will display as @[@ abcdefghijklmnopqrstuvwxyz @]@ and 
 
     ABCDEFGHIJKLMNOPQRSTUVWXYZ
will display as @[@ ABCDEFGHIJKLMNOPQRSTUVWXYZ @]@ A sequence of letters with out any space between is treated as a single identifier. Thus, unlike standard Latex , white space placed between letters may make a difference in spacing depending on your browser. For example, 
 
     a b c d e f g h i j k l m n o p q r s t u v w x y z
results in @[@ a b c d e f g h i j k l m n o p q r s t u v w x y z @]@
